Questions

Good ones, anyway.

Is there a subscription?

Free plan covers one kid and up to 6 chores — no ads, no trial countdown, no account. Bucko Pro unlocks unlimited kids and unlimited chores for A$1.99/month or A$17.99/year with a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime.

Is it on Android?

Yes — iOS and Android ship at the same time. Same app, same features, same on-device-only data. Tablets too.

How many kids can I add?

One on Free, unlimited on Pro. The layout adapts — one kid gets a huge central button, two stack side by side, three-plus go to a grid.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Back up to a file any time and restore on a new phone. iCloud (iOS) and Google Drive (Android) sync is on the way for Pro — family data travels automatically.

Can the kids use it themselves?

Yes — Kids View turns the phone into two giant tap-targets. They can't change chores or payouts. One tap puts you back in control.

What age is this for?

Best for kids roughly 4 to 13.

Do rewards have to be money?

No. Points can be anything.
Money, screen time, treats.
